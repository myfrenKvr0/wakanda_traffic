Wakanda Traffic Department

About

The purpose of this website is to provide the Traffic Department of Wakanda with a platform that empowers traffic officials with the cloud services that aid them fulfill their duties.

As such, three cloudlet services are embedded within this website. The three cloudlets, along with their purposes are outlined below.

Services


  • Customer Repository

    This cloudlet is developed to facilitate the registering and maintaining of road user information. As such, user information such as bio data, address and vehicle information is stored on a NoSQL database.

  • Violator Identification

    This cloudlet is developed to facilitate autonomous Image recognition processes. Hence, this cloudlet is capable of recognising text from images and the extraction/Recognition of number plates from images.

  • Traffic analysis and visualization

    The following cloudlet is responsible for the visualization of data, which could include times at which traffic is at peak, roads in the city etc. As such, this cloudlet is intended to visualize and provide insights on the performed analysis.
